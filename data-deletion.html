Account & Data Deletion Request

If you would like to request deletion of your Looksy account and associated data, please follow the instructions below.

Contact Information

Steps to Request Account Deletion

1. Send an email to the contact address above with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request - Looksy"
2. Include the email address associated with your Looksy account
3. Provide your username (if different from email)
4. Confirm that you understand this action is irreversible
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data including wardrobe items, journal entries, photos, and progress will be permanently deleted from our servers.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your account profile and personal information
  • All wardrobe items and photos
  • Journal entries and associated images
  • User statistics, streaks, and achievements
  • All uploaded content and metadata

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days of receiving your request. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Data Retention

After account deletion, no personal data will be retained. Some anonymized usage statistics may be kept for analytical purposes but will not be linked to your identity.
